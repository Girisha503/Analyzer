ABOUT

AI-Based Customer Complaint Summarizer and Responder is built to transform customer service using Generative AI. Powered by advanced language models like GPT-4, the tool analyzes lengthy customer complaints, generates clear summaries, and suggests polite, ready-to-use responses. Developed with Python, LangChain, and Streamlit, it helps support teams reduce workload, save time, and maintain consistent communication across industries like banking, e-commerce, and telecom. Trained on real-world complaint data, the system also uses basic sentiment analysis to better understand customer emotions.
